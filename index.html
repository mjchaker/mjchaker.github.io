01 — About

Engineer, producer, problem-solver.

I'm an engineering student at Ontario Tech University, working in software alongside my studies. I care about the whole product — not just whether the code works, but whether it's clear, correct, and built to last. My core languages are C, Python, and Scheme: low-level control when performance matters, quick iteration when it doesn't, and the functional mindset that makes both cleaner.

My background as a musician shapes how I build software. Producing a track means managing complexity, layering systems that have to work in harmony, and knowing when something is genuinely done versus merely finished. I bring that same taste and discipline to every codebase I touch.

I'm open to opportunities of all kinds — internships, roles, collaborations, engineering or music. If you're building something interesting, let's talk.
